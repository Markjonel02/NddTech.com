COMPANY PROFILE

Established in 2018, NDD Tech is a company that specializes in Auxiliary, Automation, Electrical and HVAC Contracting & subcontracting Services, continuously providing remarkable technical support and excellent solutions. The company offers various and versatile services to fulfill particular client requirements.

NDDTECH follows three CORE VALUES. Commitment, Efficiency and Innovation. The company is committed to bring out the best out of its people in order to exceed client expectations, without sacrificing the quality and efficiency of service provided. We live by the principle that there is always something new to learn, so the company is always in the search for innovative ways to serve its clients at its best.
